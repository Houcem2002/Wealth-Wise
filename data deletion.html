Data Deletion Instructions for Wealth-Wise

In accordance with Google Play, App Store, and GDPR/CCPA requirements, Wealth-Wise allows you to request the complete deletion of your personal data. Choose one of the options below to proceed with your data deletion request.

Option 1: Delete Within the App

The fastest way to delete your account and all associated data:

  1. Open the Wealth-Wise app on your device.
  2. Navigate to the Profile or Settings screen.
  3. Tap on Delete Account.
  4. Confirm your choice. Your account, along with all associated transactions, budgets, and personal data stored in our database, will be permanently deleted.

Processing time: Immediate

Option 2: Request Deletion via Email

If you no longer have access to the app, you can request data deletion by sending an email to our support team:

  • Email to: houcemcherguia1954@gmail.com
  • Subject: Account Data Deletion Request
  • Body: Please include the exact email address you used to register your account in the Wealth-Wise app so we can find and delete your data securely.

Processing time: 7-14 business days

⚠️ Important Notice: Data deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your financial records, data analytics, and account details will be wiped completely from our secure servers (Firebase Cloud Firestore and Auth). Please ensure you have backed up any information you wish to keep before proceeding with the deletion request.
